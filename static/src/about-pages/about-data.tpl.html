Publications

Impactstory imports all your Public works on ORCID that have DOIs (A DOI is a unique ID assigned to most scholarly articles, as well as many other products like datasets and figures).

Sometimes a publication might show up on your ORCID, but not on Impactstory. There are three reasons why:

  • The work isn't Public on ORCID

    Solution: make sure all your ORCID works are set to Public, like this:

  • The work has no DOI set on ORCID

    Solution: Add DOIs to your works on ORCID. You can enter these manually, but the easiest way is to re-add the works using ORCID's Scopus or DataCite importers; these will bring in the works again, but this time with DOIs.

  • The work has no DOI at all

    Solution: archive your publications in a repository get a DOI for the new, persistently-archived version. Then you can import the new DOI into ORCID and Impactstory as normal. Here's how to archive your articles, slides, datasets, and more.
